# Official Rules v0.5

- Two players; first to $50 in tips wins immediately.
- Each player starts at $0 with a bartender, a legal 30-card deck, and one switch token.
- Gain switch tokens when crossing $15, $30, and $45. Switch before drink selection.
- Reveal one customer. Draw seven cards and choose exactly three.
- Love +3, Like +2, Dislike −2, matching bartender specialty +1.
- Price never adds Appeal. Highest Appeal wins; higher price breaks a tie; remaining ties are random.
- Winner gains 25% of the served drink's price plus $2. Loser gains 10%. Payouts are rounded to whole dollars.
- Decks contain exactly 30 cards with no more than three copies of one drink.
