Non-negotiables

Production rules

  • No text, logos, prices, borders, or UI baked into character or drink illustrations.
  • Keep important faces, glass rims, and garnishes inside the 10% safe zone.
  • Use fictional adults aged 21+; never imitate a celebrity or real person.
  • No real alcohol brands, protected logos, watermarks, signatures, or readable background labels.
  • One clear focal subject, readable at 160px wide, with a distinct silhouette.
  • Warm key light always comes from upper left; cool rim light comes from camera right.
  • Export masters before compression and retain generation records for every asset.
  • Reject malformed hands, glassware, faces, stray objects, duplicate garnishes, and illegible silhouettes.
